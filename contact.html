Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register as a student? +

Click on "Student Register" in the top navigation or go to the student registration page. Fill in your details and verify your email to get started.

How do providers get verified? +

After registration, our team reviews your application and documents. You'll receive an email confirmation once verified.

What if I forget my password? +

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your email and follow the instructions to reset your password.

How long does support take to respond? +

We typically respond within 24 hours on weekdays. For urgent issues, please call our support number.
