About UniVea

Connecting Students with Trusted Campus Services

Our Story

UniVea was born from a simple observation: students struggle to find reliable services near their campuses, while local service providers struggle to reach their ideal customers.

Our mission is to bridge this gap by creating a trusted platform where students can easily discover essential services, and providers can grow their business within the student community.

🎯

Our Mission

To simplify campus life by connecting students with trusted, affordable, and convenient services right at their doorstep.

🌟

Our Vision

To become the most trusted platform for student services, creating vibrant campus communities where every need is just a click away.
